BusyBee – Productivity App

Overview

BusyBee is a bright, bee-themed productivity app that helps users stay organized, manage tasks efficiently, and maintain focus through a clean, friendly UI.

My Role

UI design, visual direction, layout, and interaction design.

Goals

  • Create a simple and friendly everyday productivity experience
  • Make organization feel light instead of overwhelming
  • Use a memorable visual identity centered around the “bee” theme

Key Decisions

  • Bold yellow palette with strong contrast for clarity
  • Rounded shapes and soft shadows for playfulness
  • Clear hierarchy so tasks and priorities are easy to scan
