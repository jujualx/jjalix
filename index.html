About Me

I’m a Senior Designer with eight years of experience, but my love for art started way before that. Creativity has always been my comfort zone—it’s what pushed me to study Arts and Design and eventually turn it into a career.

I’ve always enjoyed exploring different corners of this industry, learning and adapting as everything evolves. And honestly, what makes me happiest is creating designs that feel clear and easy for people to use—work that makes them smile because they instantly get what they’re looking at or what they need to do.
