About

Hey there! I’m Julia.

I’m currently a Senior Designer with eight years of experience, with a strong track record working with international clients across print, digital design, and web. Creativity has always been my comfort zone—it’s what pushed me to study Arts and Design and eventually turn it into a career.

I love exploring different corners of the design industry, learning and adapting as everything evolves. Right now I’m into frontend development. What makes me happiest is creating designs that feel clear and easy for people to use—work that feels natural, because they instantly get what they’re looking at or what they need to do.
