UnaMarket – Seller’s Landing Page

UI Design • Web • Landing Page

Overview

UnaMarket is a landing page concept for marketplace sellers. The goal is to make onboarding feel simple and trustworthy, while clearly presenting the benefits of selling on the platform.

My Role

UI design, layout, and visual direction — focusing on a clean structure, friendly illustration, and a strong hero section with the UnaMarket logo.

Design Goals

  • Communicate trust and simplicity for new sellers.
  • Highlight key benefits and features without overwhelming the page.
  • Use a bright, modern palette that feels connected to e-commerce.

Structure

  • Hero section with logo, tagline, and clear primary CTA.
  • Benefits section that explains why sellers should join.
  • Supportive illustration to keep the page warm and human.
